# /reflect - Retrospective

Reflect on `$ARGUMENTS` and capture learnings:

## Reflection Framework

### What Went Well
- Successful approaches
- Effective tools/techniques
- Good decisions

### What Didn't Go Well
- Challenges faced
- Mistakes made
- Time wasted

### What We Learned
- New knowledge
- Improved understanding
- Skills developed

### Action Items
- Improvements for next time
- Processes to change
- Knowledge to document

## Reflection Process

1. **Review** - Look back at recent work
2. **Analyze** - Identify patterns
3. **Document** - Capture insights
4. **Plan** - Define improvements
